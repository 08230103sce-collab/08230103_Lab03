About Me

Hello! My name is Birkha Maya Rai. Currently, I am a trainee at Samtse College of Education, pursuing a B.Ed in ICT.

I come from Sarpang and belong to a middle-class family that values education and community service. I am the second eldest of four siblings. My elder brother completed his tertiary education at JNEC, and my younger brother and sister are still studying. My family has always supported my education and my aspiration to become a teacher. I completed my schooling at Sarpang LSS (2018), Sarpang Central School (2021), and Rinchen HSS (2022).

Currently, I am exploring my interests in Information and Communication Technology, making new friendships, and preparing for my future career in teaching where I hope to make a positive impact on students' lives.
